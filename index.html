A 5-step guide to the creamiest and most authentic Roman pasta

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale
  • 3 large eggs
  • 50g grated Pecorino Romano
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt for pasta water

Step 2: Cook the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta water before draining.

Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano, and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper. This forms the creamy base of your carbonara‚Äîno cream needed!

Step 4: Saut√© the Pancetta

In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the pancetta until it becomes crispy and golden. Turn off the heat but leave the pan warm for mixing the pasta later.

Step 5: Combine and Serve

Quickly toss the hot pasta with the pancetta in the warm pan. Slowly pour in the egg mixture while stirring constantly to create a creamy sauce. Add some reserved pasta water if needed to loosen it. Serve immediately with extra Pecorino Romano and freshly ground black pepper.
